Web & Business Strategy, Design & Development

Objective

Create a new website that reflects Amanda Mason’s business values and mission of helping others change their lives through the means of fitness and nutrition, as well as set new systems that are more efficient in running the business and engage new and current clients. Main goals:

  • Refinement of the brand identity so it clearly communicates the heart and soul of the business
  • Beautiful website design, including blog and e-commerce via affiliate links
  • Easy to upload new content and maintain
  • Set-up of an efficient payment system, as well as client onboarding and managing processes
  • Simple and strategically-thought navigation that improves conversion rates

What's included

  • One-on-one consultancy and strategic assistance throughout the process to help translate your vision and identify processes, via email or Skype (max. 30-min weekly sessions advisable)
  • Research, consultancy and support on setting up automation systems that ease the flow of running the business
  • Questionnaire about your brand, references and goals for your website
  • Custom designed website, including blog AND e-commerce (via affiliate links) set-up. After evaluating alternatives and preferences, the website will be:
    • Built from scratch in HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and Jekyll, integrated to a user-friendly Content Management System (CMS) - CloudCannon - to update content, OR
    • Fully customised and set-up on Squarespace or Wordpress
  • 7 web pages including: About, Contact, Retreats, Services, Challenge, Blog and Online Shop
  • Responsive across all devices, including mobile and tablet
  • Optimised for speed and SEO
  • Upload of all texts and contents for launch
  • Domain integration
  • Personalised tutorial of how to use your CMS or website platform to update and upload your content in the future
  • Google Analytics integration
  • Mailchimp integration, including emailing template design and list set-up
  • Refinement of current logo
  • Gmail for Business set-up with domain extension, including support on creation of canned responses

Notes

  • All content, images and texts to be provided by the client before the start of the project
  • Does not include hosting or Squarespace fees, to be agreed and advised when choosing the website platform
  • Website maintenance fee available on request, after agreeing on the website platform

Process

1.

Let's start

In your case, once we decide on the website building platform that best suits your needs, I'll send you the first invoice, the project questionnaire and a friendly contract. When everything is ready, I'll lock you in my calendar and we'll get hands-on with your project.

2.

Strategy: Let's get clear

5 working days

I’ll send you a personalised questionnaire that will help us be clear on the goals and functionalities of your business and website, as well as design references that we both have. After a 30-min Skype strategy call, we'll have a clear idea of the business needs and design inspiration for the website.

3.

Design: Let’s make it beautiful

5 working days

With the goals and inspiration, I’ll refine your current logo and prepare a design mock-up of the website for you to review.

4.

Development: Let’s make it real

10 working days

After you approve the design, I’ll build the website will all the features and functionalities. I’ll put special emphasis on leaving room for you to add more and/or different content or sections and I will suggest blog categories that will contribute to better positioning on search engines.

5.

Review & Upload: Let's finalise it

5 working days

I’ll add all your content and transfer the old blog posts, and upload the website on a hidden domain for you to review. I’ll also prepare a tutorial to teach you to update and upload content in the future (in case you don’t purchase a maintenance add-on).

6.

Systems set-up: Let’s make it smooth

5 working days

I’ll set up Google Analytics, Mailchimp, Gmail for Business and a payment automation system. I’ll map out a client onboarding system that helps lock in more customers and ease your workload, including the suggestion of canned responses and email automations on Mailchimp.
